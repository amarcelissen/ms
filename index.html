My Skills.

3D Design

I do 3D designs using Fusion 360 .



3D Printing Software

I use Simplify 3D software to slice the models I design to produce the best possible components that fit together accurately.

3D Printing

I use eight 3D printers (up to 400mm cube size) to produce the various components that are assembled for the complete prototypes, often producing 100 or more completed samples for field testing.

Finishing & Assembly

All componets are sanded smooth and fitted together as carefully as possible. Parts are then painted to give them a professional apperance.
